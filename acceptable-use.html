Acceptable Use Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes what you may and may not do when using LucyNotes (the "Service"). It is part of our Terms of Service. We may update it from time to time. Violating this AUP can result in content removal, account suspension, or termination, and may be reported to authorities where required.

You agree NOT to use the Service to:

1. Break the law or infringe rights

  • Upload, share, or generate content that infringes anyone's copyright, trademark, or other intellectual-property rights (e.g. posting full copyrighted textbooks, slides, or articles you don't have rights to share).
  • Violate any applicable law or regulation.

2. Record people without required consent

  • Record any lecture, conversation, or individual where doing so is prohibited by law, by your institution, or without the consent required in your jurisdiction. Several U.S. states require all parties to consent. You are solely responsible for obtaining necessary consent before recording, including from professors and classmates.

3. Violate others' privacy

  • Upload or share another person's private, personal, or sensitive information without their permission.
  • Share another student's or educator's work or content without authorization.

4. Post harmful or abusive content

  • Harass, bully, threaten, defame, or impersonate others.
  • Upload or share content that is hateful, violent, sexually explicit, or otherwise objectionable, especially in community or school spaces.
  • Facilitate cheating or academic dishonesty in violation of your institution's policies.

5. Endanger minors

  • Upload, request, or share any content that sexualizes or endangers minors. We report such content to the appropriate authorities.

6. Attack or misuse the Service

  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service, other users' accounts or content, or our systems.
  • Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service except under an authorized disclosure process (see our security contact).
  • Interfere with or disrupt the Service, including by uploading malware, overloading our systems, scraping, or sending automated or excessive requests.
  • Circumvent usage limits, access controls, subscription tiers, or rate limits, or misrepresent your identity, account type, or entitlements.
  • Reverse-engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract source code or model prompts, except where permitted by law.

7. Misuse AI features

  • Use the AI assistant to generate content that violates this AUP (illegal content, harassment, infringement, etc.).
  • Attempt to manipulate the AI (e.g. prompt injection) to bypass safeguards or exfiltrate other users' data.

8. Commercial misuse

  • Resell, sublicense, or commercially exploit the Service or other users' content without our written permission.

Reporting violations

If you see content or behavior that violates this AUP, report it to info@lucynotes.com. For copyright specifically, use the process in our DMCA Policy. For security vulnerabilities, contact info@lucynotes.com (or our published security contact).

Enforcement

We may, at our discretion and consistent with law: remove or disable content, issue warnings, limit features, suspend or terminate accounts (including for repeat infringers), and cooperate with law enforcement. We are not obligated to monitor content but may do so.
