Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lucy Notes LLC ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the LucyNotes mobile application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

We built LucyNotes for students. We try to collect as little as we can, and we never sell your personal information.

1. Who we are

The Service is operated by Lucy Notes LLC. For any privacy question or request, contact us at info@lucynotes.com.

2. Information we collect

a) Information you provide

  • Account information — your email address and password (passwords are handled by our authentication provider and stored only as a salted hash; we never see your plaintext password).
  • Profile information — display name, and optionally your school, major, and account role (e.g. student/educator).
  • Content you create or upload — audio recordings of lectures, photos of boards/slides you capture, PDFs and documents you upload, the transcripts and study notes generated from them, flashcards, and your chat conversations with our AI assistant ("Lucy").
  • Community content — notes, documents, and messages you choose to share to a school or study group.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics — events such as starting a recording, creating a note, sending a chat message, opening flashcards, and downloading a PDF, along with basic context (e.g. app screen, device type). We use these to understand which features help students.
  • Device & push information — a push-notification token (if you enable notifications) and general device/OS information.
  • Diagnostic/crash data — error and performance logs to help us fix bugs.

c) We do NOT intentionally collect

  • Government IDs, payment card numbers (any future payments are processed by Apple/Google or a PCI-compliant processor — we never receive full card numbers), or precise geolocation.

3. A note about recordings

LucyNotes lets you record lectures. You are responsible for obtaining any consent required by your school and by the laws of your state before recording. Some U.S. states require that all parties to a conversation consent to being recorded. The app shows a consent reminder before your first recording, but that reminder is not legal advice. Do not record where it is prohibited.

4. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the core Service — transcribe your recordings, generate notes, answer your questions, and sync your content across your devices.
  • Operate community/school sharing features you choose to use.
  • Maintain, secure, debug, and improve the Service.
  • Communicate with you about the Service (e.g. account, security, and — only if you opt in — product updates).
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms.

We do not use your private notes, recordings, or chats to train our own advertising profiles, and we do not sell your personal information.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights or users' safety, or in connection with a merger or acquisition (with notice as required by law).

6. Data retention

We keep your account content until you delete it or close your account. When you delete a note, recording, or chat, we remove it from your device and request deletion from cloud storage. Backups and provider logs may persist for a limited period before being overwritten. Diagnostic and analytics data is retained for a limited period for security and product analysis.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any right, email info@lucynotes.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law and will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

  • Account deletion — you can request full deletion of your account and associated content at any time; this cascades across our database, storage, analytics, and diagnostics providers.
  • Data export — you can request a copy of your content in a portable format.
  • Analytics/notifications — you can disable push notifications in your device settings; analytics opt-out controls are provided in the app where required.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the rights described above, including the right to know what we collect and the right to deletion. We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under California law. We do not offer financial incentives in exchange for personal information.

EEA/UK (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, our legal bases for processing are: performance of a contract (to provide the Service), legitimate interests (to secure and improve the Service), consent (e.g. optional analytics/marketing where required), and legal obligation. You may lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

8. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact info@lucynotes.com and we will delete it. Where a user is a minor under the age of majority, a parent, guardian, or school may need to consent on their behalf.

9. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, access controls, and on-device secure storage for credentials. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and to notify you of breaches as required by law.

10. International transfers

We are based in the United States and process data there. If you use the Service from outside the U.S., you understand your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other countries where our sub-processors operate, with appropriate safeguards where required.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. When we make material changes we will update the "Effective date" and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Your continued use after an update means you accept the revised Policy.
